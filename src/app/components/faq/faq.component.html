FAQ

What are CRC cards?

Class-responsibility-collaboration (CRC) cards are a brainstorming tool used in the design of object-oriented software.

How do you use CRC cards?

The starting point is to think of the nouns that exist in your system. From there, you can assign responsibilities (methods) to each object, as well as the collaborators (other objects) that are needed to fulfill those functionalities.

How do I use this tool?

Fill in each card's information with its class name, responsibilities, and collaborators. You may add more cards or reset all cards if you wish to start over. You can then visualize your compact cards in a free space by dragging and dropping them.
